    5 Fascinating Facts About Birdsong

  • The songs of many bird species are highly complex and can contain dozens of notes per second.
  • Songbirds of the same species may have different dialectsdepending on their geographical area.
  • Birds aren’t born knowing their population’s songs. Just like humans, they have to listen to adults singing to pick up on the “language."
  • In most cases, when you hear a bird singing, you’re probably hearing a male.
  • Several songbird species don’t sing only their own tunes, but appropriate the tunes of other species as well.
